Everything your wallet can't do

One tap to your card

Open the app, tap the store, and the barcode fills the screen — ready to scan.

Scan to add

Point your camera at the physical card's barcode and it's saved in seconds.

Brand-accurate

Each card shows in the store's real colours and logo, so it's easy to spot.

Local + worldwide

Pick your country and see the shops that matter to you — plus global brands.

Private by default

Cards are stored on your device. No sign-up, no tracking, works offline.

Share your cards

Send a card — or your whole set — to family or friends with a link, so everyone can scan the same barcode.

Up and running in under a minute

  1. 1

    Open the app

    It's a web app — nothing to download. Add it to your home screen to launch it full-screen like a native app.

  2. 2

    Add your cards

    Pick a store and type or scan the number. Repeat for every card in your wallet.

  3. 3

    Show & collect

    At the till, open the card and hold up the barcode. That's it — points collected.
